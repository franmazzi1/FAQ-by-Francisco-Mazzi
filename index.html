How Does the Refund Process Work?

  1. Request a Refund: To start a refund, log in to your account and go to the support section. Find the product you want to return and select the "Request a Refund" option.
  2. Meet the Requirements: Refunds are eligible if: The product was purchased less than 14 days ago. You have played for less than 2 hours in the case of games.
  3. Review Process: Once requested, we will review your refund request and notify you if it meets the refund conditions.
  4. Confirmation and Refund: If your refund is approved, the money will be reimbursed to your original payment method or as credit to your account, depending on your preference. Processing time may vary depending on the selected payment method.
  5. Exceptions: Some products, such as in-game purchases or consumed downloadable content, may not be eligible for a refund.
